Our Story & Mission

Reimagining educational technology through gamified pathways, engagement-first methods, and community competition.

What is EduQuests?

EduQuests is an innovative educational platform built to transform the standard academic curriculum into a series of rewarding quests. Instead of reading endless chapters and sitting through passive video lectures, users learn by solving interactive problems, compiling solutions, and writing active sandboxed code.

We believe that education shouldn't feel like a chore. By blending behavioral game design mechanics—such as experience points (XP), learning streaks, leveling structures, and leaderboard mechanics—with modern pedagogy, we help students build persistent habits and master skills faster.
